A Soft Beginning

Before the Story Continues

There are feelings that cannot be contained inside one message, one photograph, or one ordinary birthday wish.

They need room to breathe — between old paper, pressed flowers, quiet pauses, and pages turned slowly.

7

My Feelings

Some Feelings Never Leave

I don’t even know where to begin because no matter how much time passes, some feelings never completely leave the heart. And maybe that’s why today feels a little heavier than usual.

I used to imagine this day so differently. I had plans for you, so many plans. I had already thought about the gifts I wanted to buy, the little things I wanted to surprise you with, and the smile I wanted to see on your face.

8

The Plans I Carried

Everything I Wanted to Do

I had been saving money slowly, quietly, just so I could make your birthday special in the way you deserved. Every time I saw something that reminded me of you, I’d think, “He would love this.”

And it made me happy, because loving you made even small things feel meaningful. You’ll probably never realize how much love was hidden behind the things I never got the chance to do for you.

9

What I Wanted for You

I Wanted to Celebrate You

I wanted to celebrate you. I wanted to make you feel loved, important, and cared for. I wanted to be the reason your birthday felt warm and unforgettable.

But life had other plans for us. And maybe that’s what hurts the most—not just losing you, but losing all the moments I had already created in my heart for us.

10

What You Became to Me

More Than a Memory

Out of all the people I’ve met in life, you were the one who truly touched my soul in a way I can never fully explain.

Even if we are no longer together, a part of me will always carry you quietly within it. Some people become memories, but some become a part of your heart forever.

You became both.

11

A Part of Every Day

You Were Never Just a Person

You know, people come and go all the time, but you were never “just a person” to me. You became a habit, a comfort, a safe place, and a part of my everyday thoughts.

I loved you in ways I never knew I was capable of loving someone—so deeply, so honestly, so softly. Even now, after everything, I still catch myself caring about whether you’re eating properly, sleeping on time, smiling genuinely, or silently hurting behind your strong face.

12

What Never Changed

My Prayers for You

I just want you to know that despite everything, my prayers for you have never changed.

I still pray that life becomes gentle with you. I pray that your dreams come true, that your heart heals from every pain it hides, and that one day you wake up feeling genuinely happy and loved.

13

For the Life Ahead

Everything I Still Wish for You

You deserve a future filled with peace, success, laughter, and people who never make you question your worth.

I hope you become everything you once told me you wanted to become. And when life gets hard, I hope you remember there was once someone who loved you so deeply that even after losing you, they still couldn’t stop wishing the world for you.

14

What Loving You Taught Me

I’ll Never Regret Loving You

Loving you taught me how deeply a heart can feel, how attached a soul can become, and how painful it is to miss someone who still means so much to you.

A part of me will probably always look for you in songs, in late-night thoughts, in late-night calls and video calls, and in the songs we sang together.

15

The Memories That Return

In All the Little Places

I will probably always look for you in the places we went together or planned to visit, in the goals we imagined for the future, and in little memories that suddenly return without warning.

And maybe you’ll never fully realise how much you mattered to me, but you did—more than words could ever explain.

16

On Your Birthday

Please Take Care of Yourself

So on your birthday, I just want to say—please take care of yourself for me too. Eat properly, sleep on time, work hard for your dreams, and never let the world make your heart cold....You have such a beautiful soul, Peeyush. Don't ever lose it. No matter where life takes us, a part of me will always love you silently, from afar.

Happy Birthday, Mera Bachcha 🐰

What You Meant to Me

Impossible to Forget

I don’t think you’ll ever truly understand how special you are to the people who love you—especially to me. There was always something about you that made you impossible to forget. Maybe it was your gentleness, the softness in your heart, the way you cared without even trying, or simply the comfort your presence gave me. You had this calmness in you that could make even the worst days feel lighter.

🍀
19

The Things I Loved

Your Gentleness, Your Heart

I loved your gentleness. I loved your heart. I loved the way you spoke, the way you cared, and the way your presence could calm my overthinking mind. I loved your smile more than you’ll ever know. Even your flaws became precious to me because they were yours. There was something so beautiful about you, Peeyush— something that made my soul feel attached to yours in a way that still scares me.

20

Your Beautiful Nature

Pure and Rare

Honestly, one of the most beautiful things about you was your nature. The way you spoke, the way you listened, and the way you loved—it all felt so pure and rare. You never realised how deeply your small efforts, your attention, your words, and your care affected someone’s heart.

But they did. Mine especially.

❦

The Person You Are

A Handsome Soul

You’re genuinely one of the most handsome souls I’ve ever known—not only because of the way you look, but because of the person you are. Your smile, your eyes, your voice, and your way of expressing things all carried warmth. Even your flaws felt beautiful because they made you real Maybe that’s why moving on from you never felt easy— because people like you don’t come twice in someone’s life.
